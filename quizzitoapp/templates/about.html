About Us

This site was created to help developers learning how to program test their knowledge in the field they are pursuing.

The website has quiz on various programming laguages which give our users the ability to test themselves in more than one programming language.

The quizes has also been structured in a way that the user starts from a beginner level(quiz 1-4) and to the intermediate level(quiz 5-8) and then advance level(quiz 9-10).
